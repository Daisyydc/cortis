CORTIS

CORTIS (코르티스), formerly known as BIGHITNBG (BIGHIT New Boy Group), is a 5-member South Korean boy group under BIGHIT MUSIC. The members are Martin, James, Juhoon, Seonghyeon, and Keonho. In the USA, they’re represented by Republic Records. They debuted on August 18, 2025, with their first single, “What You Want”.

Group Name Meaning: It’s an acronym for COLOR OUTSIDE THE LINES. It signifies “thinking freely, breaking free from the world’s established standards and rules.”

Fandom Name Meaning: The name brings together “COR” (with CORTIS) & “~ER” (those who stand together), while also echoing “CORE” – the fans who support CORTIS with the deepest love and passion. The name symbolizes the core driving force behind CORTIS & those who will “color outside the lines” together with CORTIS.
